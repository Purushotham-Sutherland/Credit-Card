IBAN Validation – Document Guidelines

Front Side
  • Show holder’s name and full IBAN.
  • Card digits must be partially or fully covered.
  • Hide middle digits.
Back Side
  • IBAN must be fully visible.

Note: If both IBAN and card digits appear, accept as credit card or bank book—ensure correct doc type and mask required card digits.
